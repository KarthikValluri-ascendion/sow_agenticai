## Parties

**Client:** ClientCo  
**Vendor:** Vendor LLC  
**Effective date:** [DATE]  
**Term:** 14 weeks from kickoff.

## 1. Purpose

Vendor will deliver a **minimum viable** consumer mobile application for iOS and Android for Client’s Phoenix pilot region. Work is **time-boxed** and optimized for **fast execution** and **rapid scaling** of the pilot user base.

## 2. Scope

- Implement agreed user stories in **two-week sprints** with a fixed Vendor team.
- Integrate with Client’s test APIs; production cutover is out of scope.
- **No** enterprise data warehouse, **no** long-term managed-services commitment, and **no** standing ethics board or standing architecture review body unless Client schedules ad hoc sessions.

## 3. Deliverables

Working builds per sprint, release notes, and a single handover demo at the end of week 14.

## 4. Governance

Weekly 30-minute status call. Issues are tracked in Client’s tool. **Dynamic planning modules and automated anomaly detection are explicitly out of scope.** Vendor does **not** deploy sprint velocity dashboards, resource burn analytics, or continuous automated anomaly detection as part of this SOW.

## 5. Fees

Fixed fee for the pilot; details in Schedule A.

## 6. Assumptions

Client provides timely access to test environments. **Neither party** commits to weekly client sentiment studies, formal “ethics gate” intake artifacts, or pre-kickoff automated compliance scanning in this document.
